Path to Neutral Understanding

While the rhetorical approaches differ significantly—with one side emphasizing "Universal Rights" and the other "Market Choice"—the data reveals a functional consensus on cost transparency and waste reduction.

A productive neutral path involves reframing the debate away from who pays to what we are paying for. Focusing legislative efforts on standardized billing practices offers the highest probability of bi-partisan support (85% likelihood) without triggering ideological red lines on government mandate vs. private enterprise.
